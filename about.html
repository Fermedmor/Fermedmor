WORK PROCESS

Process



Consultation Call
Free 30 min Virtual Meeting

First I like to meet my clients and hear their problems to see if we would be a good fit. Sometimes they only need some guidance and direction to other resources.



Discovery & Alignment
1hr call

Then, if we're a good fit and I can help them solve their problem we outline together what they want to get out of the project, the timeline , deliverables and final pricing.



Branding
30 min
Virtual Meeting

Afterwards, I present the client with branding elements such as ideal customer, user journey and look & feel of the brand. The client gives feedback and I use this as my foundation for the deliverables.



Creative Drafts
30 min
Virtual Meeting

If the customer needs rebranding assets such as a logo for example, I present them with 3 different boards to see which one fits their brand best and then I create all the final products based on that board.



Final Deliverables
30 min
Virtual Meeting

I present the client with the final deliverables such as videos, campaigns, logos, fonts, ads, website, pictures, emails and ask for feedback on their experience.

Training
30-6O min
Virtual Meeting

IF DESIRED I provide the client with any training required to succeed with the marketing I've helped with. For example showing them how to edit the website without hiring another marketer
