The Power of First Impressions

As the lead marketer for a student orientation program, I was responsible for developing and executing a comprehensive marketing strategy that would attract and engage new students. Drawing on my experience in marketing and strategic planning, I implemented several new processes that resulted in the adoption of new technologies and an increase in engagement from students.

One of the key initiatives I led was the implementation of SMS marketing and email marketing campaigns, which helped us to reach students in real-time and keep them informed about important events and deadlines. I worked closely with our IT team to develop and implement these campaigns, ensuring that they were tailored to the specific needs and preferences of our student population.

In addition to these new technologies, I was also responsible for all of the marketing content produced for the orientation program, including graphics and other visual elements. I worked closely with our creative team to develop materials that were engaging, informative, and visually appealing, while also staying true to the overall branding and messaging of the program.

Thanks to these efforts, we were able to significantly increase engagement from new students, with higher attendance rates at events and increased participation in orientation activities. Feedback from students was overwhelmingly positive, with many citing the effectiveness and convenience of the new SMS and email campaigns. Overall, I'm proud of the role I played in developing and executing a successful marketing strategy for RockVIU new student orientation.
